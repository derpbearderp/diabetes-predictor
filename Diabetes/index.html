Early stage diabetes predictor

Welcome to the early stage diabetes predictor.
This application is intended to help sick people with an unknown illness determine if they might be suffering from diabetes or not.
By filling out the fields on this website our team can predict whether you might be showing early signs of diabetes. Keep in mind that if you are healthy the results won't be completely accurate
To use this application all you need to do is fill out all fields.
Read the instructions in the information bubbles to know what to input in each field. Make sure to input the right data as it will influence the results
Our predictions are made based on the data from this dataset
